Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. Your profile, saved items, and search history are stored locally on your device. We don't create user accounts and don't track you personally. If you opt into anonymous data sharing, we collect quality metrics about AI results (including your search query text) to improve the App — but this data contains no personal identifiers and you can opt out at any time. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Are the quotes accurate?

Invoke Wisdom uses AI to find and present wisdom. While we optimize for accuracy, AI-generated content may contain errors or misattributions. We recommend verifying quotes before using them in formal or published contexts.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings in the App and tap "Delete All My Data." This removes all your saved items, profile information, and search history. Uninstalling the App also removes all local data.

Why am I seeing a search limit?

Invoke Wisdom includes a daily search limit to manage costs and ensure service quality. The limit resets each day.
